Curriculum

Junior Schol is Christ-centered

The curriculum in SDH Kindergarten is Christ-centered, Bible-based, student-oriented, teacher-directed, and community-connected. Our Kindergarten uses national curriculum, the 2013 national curriculum. We also develop an SDH Curriculum Framework that enriches the national curriculum and has made the SDH curriculum unique.

SDH Curriculum Framework uses an authentic and holistic learning approach which direct the teaching and learning toward the school vision and mission.

Our Community

Life Long Learner's Community

Community is a community which consists of Christ’s disciples, where teachers, staffs and other workers are called by God to be a learner, not only in this world but also to be a long life learners.

To be involved in Bintang Mulia School community means that we will be a long life learner, that is every student who has been entrusted to us will involve in a community who have desire and calling to know, walk with, and love God and His word.
